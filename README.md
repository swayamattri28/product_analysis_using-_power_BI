# Product Analysis using Power BI
## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing product performance and business insights using **Power BI**, **SQL**, and **Excel**.  
The objective was to understand product sales trends, customer preferences, and performance metrics across regions.
---
## Objectives
- Identify top-performing and underperforming products.
- Analyze region-wise and category-wise sales distribution.
- Create interactive dashboards for business decision-making.
- Track KPIs such as revenue, profit margin, and customer growth.
---
## Tech Stack
- **Power BI:** Data visualization and dashboard creation  
- **SQL:** Data extraction, cleaning, and aggregation  
- **Excel:** Data preprocessing and exploratory analysis  
---
## Dataset
The dataset contains sales data with columns such as:
- `Product ID`
- `Category`
- `Region`
- `Sales`
- `Profit`
- `Customer Segment`
- `Date`
---
## Dashboard Highlights
- Interactive visuals built in **Power BI**
- Dynamic filters for product category and region
- KPI cards showing total revenue, profit, and sales count
- Trend lines visualizing monthly performance
---
## Insights
- Certain product categories drive over **60% of total revenue**
- Regional imbalance observed in sales distribution  
- Profit margins correlate strongly with customer segments and seasons
---
